UNDERSTANDING THE BUSINESS & THE DATA AT HAND¶

The analysis dataset consists of prices of Houses in King County, Washington from sales.Along, with house price it consists of information on house features, ranging from date of Sale and ID of sale to the various attributes of the different houses, that can be considered as key selling points.

Some salient points regarding the business and the business problem are,¶

Buying and selling of Real Estate and Housing in general are major drivers of a countrys economic growth

It is said that a growth of a country can be said by the rate at which construction activity occurs in it, A good example would be Chinas practice over the last decades of constructing GHOST CITIES to artificially inflate the countrys economic growth figures.

There are more than two million real estate agents in the United States.

The median sales price for houses bought in September 2019 was $ 299,400.

701,000 new houses were sold in the United States this year.(VALUE= $210 BILLION!)

73% of realtors use digital platforms for their work.

The Business Problem¶

Buying a house is commonly the most important financial transaction for the average person.

Most people buying houses are inexperienced amateurs with limited information about the market.

They tend to believe that prices will consistently rise, therefore, pay more than the house is worth.

In the US Market The mean expected rise in price was 38%. The actual rise in prices was 5.7%.

The fact that each transaction is negotiated individually creates an environment that results in an inefficient system.

What is in our Data?¶

The variables in our data set and what they convey to us is listed below,

Id-Unique ID for each home sold

Date-Date of the home sale

Price-Price of each home sold

Bedrooms- Number of bed rooms

Bathrooms- Number of bathrooms, where .5 accounts for a room with a toilet but no shower

Sqft_living- Square footage of the apartments interior living space

Sqft_lot- Square footage of the land space

Floors- Number of oors

Waterfront-A variable for whether the apartment was overlooking a waterfront or not

View- An index from 0 to 4 of how good the view from the property is

Condition- An index from 1 to 5 on the condition of the apartment,

Grade- An index from 1 to 13, where 1-3 falls short of building construction and design, 7 has an average level of construction and design, and 11-13 have a high-quality level of construction and design

Sqft_above-The square footage of the interior housing space that is above ground level

Sqft_basement-The square footage of the interior housing space that is below ground level

Yr_built-The year the house was initially built

Yr_renovated -The year of the houses last renovation

Zipcode-What zip code area the house is in, like pin code

Latitude, Latitude- coordinates of house location

Sqft_living15- The square footage of interior housing living space for the nearest 15 neighbors

Sqft_lotl5- The square footage of the land lots of the nearest 15 neighbors
